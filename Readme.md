# DormSphere

### AI-Powered Student Living Ecosystem

DormSphere is a comprehensive student-focused platform designed to simplify hostel life by bringing essential services into a single intelligent ecosystem.

Students often rely on multiple apps, websites, and chat groups to manage expenses, find study partners, recover lost items, buy and sell essentials, track laundry, check notices, and communicate with fellow residents. DormSphere solves this fragmentation by providing a centralized platform built specifically for hostel communities.

---

## Features

### DormAI

An AI-powered hostel companion that helps students with:

* Budget planning
* Study guidance
* Hostel-related queries
* Roommate issues
* Daily student life assistance

### Budget Buddy

Track expenses, monitor spending habits, and manage monthly budgets efficiently.

### MessMeter

Rate hostel meals, share reviews, and discover the community's opinion on daily food quality.

### Lost & Found

Report and search for lost belongings within the hostel community.

### Study Circles

Create and join collaborative study groups for exams, projects, and academic discussions.

### Marketplace

Buy and sell books, calculators, lab equipment, hostel essentials, and more.

### Notice Board

Access important hostel announcements, maintenance alerts, and event updates.

### Laundry Tracker

Track laundry submissions, pickup schedules, and washing status.

### Roommate Match

Find compatible roommates based on lifestyle preferences, study habits, cleanliness, and social behavior.

### Multi-Hostel Support

Students can personalize their experience by selecting:

* College
* Hostel
* Room Number

This allows DormSphere to scale across different institutions and hostel communities.

## Problem Statement

Hostel students often face challenges such as:

* Managing expenses
* Finding study partners
* Recovering lost items
* Tracking notices
* Managing laundry schedules
* Accessing reliable hostel information

These tasks are usually spread across multiple platforms, leading to inefficiency and communication gaps.

DormSphere addresses these issues by providing a unified platform that improves organization, collaboration, and student well-being.
